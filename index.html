Win-Win in Lung Mei Beach

Marine Life and Human

We are undergraduate students from the Hong Kong Polytechnic University. We currently collaborate with the Ocean Park Conservation Foundation to raise the public's awareness of man-made beaches. Our goal is to reduce the human influence on marine life in Lung Mei Beach, a man-made beach that opened on 23th of June 2021.
